Airfix 1/72 Supermarine Spitfire Mk.I

Brand: Airfix · Scale: 1/72 · Status: in progress · Started: July 2026

Unboxing

Crisp moulding with almost no flash on the sprues. The panel lines are finely recessed, which bodes well for a wash later.

The decal sheet includes markings for two Battle of Britain aircraft.

Cockpit

Interior green base coat, then a dark wash to pick out the moulded detail. The seat gets leather brown straps painted on, since the kit provides none.

Fuselage

The fuselage halves went together with only a little filler needed at the spine. Taped and left to cure overnight.
